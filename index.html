Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 6, 2025

Top Games Studio we created the mobile game Bubble Shooter Extra as a free-to-play entertainment app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require registration or personal information (name, email, phone) to play. However, the app and integrated third-party services may automatically collect certain non-personal data to operate and improve the game:

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Device & App DataDevice model, OS version, app version, language, countryCompatibility, troubleshooting, crash reports
Advertising IdentifiersGoogle Advertising ID (GAID)Deliver relevant ads, limit ad frequency
Usage Data & AnalyticsGame sessions, in-game events, crash logsImprove gameplay, balance, and stability
Network InfoIP address (short-term), connection typeAd serving and fraud detection

We do not collect contacts, photos, precise location (GPS), or microphone/camera data.

2. Third-Party Services

The game may use third-party services with their own privacy policies, such as:

  • Google AdMob – used to display ads. https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Unity Engine – runtime required to run the game. https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Analytics providers (e.g., Unity Analytics, Firebase) for crash and usage statistics.

3. How We Use Information

We use data to operate, maintain, and improve the game; provide and measure ads; detect fraud/abuse; and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal information for direct marketing.

4. Children’s Privacy

The game is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Parents or guardians who believe their child provided personal information should contact us so we can delete it.

5. Data Retention & Security

We retain data only as necessary. We implement reasonable safeguards but cannot guarantee absolute security of any data transmitted online.

6. Your Choices

  • Opt-out of personalized ads on Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt-out of Ads Personalization.
  • Uninstall the app to stop data collection by the app on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted at this URL and (when applicable) within the app.
